# Privacy Policy for Cooksy: Recipe App

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

## Your Privacy Matters

At Cooksy, we believe your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to your information.

## Information We Collect

**We don't collect any personal information.** Cooksy operates entirely on your device with no backend servers or data transmission.

## How Your Data is Stored

All app data, including:
- Saved recipes
- Favorites
- Personal notes
- App preferences

...is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this information.

## Data Sharing

We don't share your data with anyone because we never collect or have access to it in the first place.

## Third-Party Services

Cooksy does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any data, Cooksy is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be removed from your device.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on our app store listing.
